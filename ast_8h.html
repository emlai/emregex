reChar 

An ASCII character or a token, stored in ch.

A sequence of regex nodes, stored in elems and elemcount.

reStar 

A regex star operation, the operand is stored in operand.

reOpt 

A regex optional operation ('?'), operand is in operand.

reRange 

A bracket-delimited range of values, e.g. [a-z], operands in lowerbound and upperbound.

reRange2 

A bracket-delimited range of explicitly listed characters, e.g. [aeiou], the null-terminated character list in alternatives.
